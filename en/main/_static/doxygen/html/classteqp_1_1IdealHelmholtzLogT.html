Detailed Description

\( \alpha^{\rm ig}= a\times ln(T) \)

which should be compared with the original form in GERG (and REFPROP and CoolProp)

\( \alpha^{\rm ig}= a^*\ln(\tau) \)

with \(\tau=T_r/T \)
