\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Rodolfo Espíndola-\/\+Heredia, Fernando del Río and Anatol Malijevsky Optimized equation of the state of the square-\/well fluid of variable range based on a fourth-\/order free-\/energy expansion J. Chem. Phys. 130, 024509 (2009); \href{https://doi.org/10.1063/1.3054361}{\texttt{ https\+://doi.\+org/10.\+1063/1.\+3054361}}

\[
     V(r) = \left\lbrace \begin{array}{cc}
         \infty & r < \sigma \\
         -\varepsilon & \sigma < r < \lambda\sigma \\
         0 & r > \lambda \sigma
         \end{array}\right.
\]

Note\+: if needed, all the terms that don\textquotesingle{}t depend on T or rho could be pre-\/calculated at model initialization for a small speed boost
