Detailed Description

A single sigma profile. In this data structure (for consistency with the 2005 Virginia Tech database of COSMO-SAC parameters), the first column is the electron density in [e/A^2], and the second column is the probability of finding a segment with this electron density, multiplied by the segment area, in A^2
